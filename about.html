About Eloria

We believe in timeless design, responsible craftsmanship, and a touch of everyday poetry. Eloria was born from a love for beautiful, sustainable objects that tell a story.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Eloria began as a small atelier with a simple vision: to create pieces that transcend seasons and trends. Our name is inspired by the word “elegance” and the idea of a radiant, timeless aura — Eloria is the light you carry with you.

From our studio in New York, we collaborate with artisans around the world to bring you curated collections that balance modern minimalism with heritage techniques. Every piece is thoughtfully designed to be cherished for years, not just for a season.

Craftsmanship & Team

Behind every Eloria piece is a network of skilled artisans, designers, and dreamers. Our core team is based in New York, but our heart spans across Portugal, Japan, and Mexico — where we work with small-batch producers who share our dedication to quality.

  • Design: In‑house studio in Brooklyn, NY.
  • Materials: Sourced from certified sustainable suppliers.
  • Production: Small‑batch manufacturing to reduce waste.
  • Packaging: 100% recyclable and plastic‑free.

Our Sustainability Pledge

We believe that luxury and responsibility can coexist. That’s why we are constantly working to reduce our environmental footprint:

  • Carbon‑neutral shipping — we offset all deliveries.
  • Eco‑friendly materials — organic cotton, recycled metals, and biodegradable fibers.
  • Zero‑waste packaging — every box is designed to be reused or composted.
  • Tree planting — for every order, we plant a tree with our reforestation partners.

We are proud to be a Climate Neutral Certified brand, and we’re always looking for new ways to do better.
